KNOW THE CLUBS


ASTRONOMY

The CoEP Astronomy club, an ambitious team driven by amateur astronomers, has managed to conjure up a lot of interest amongst students in the science of stargazing.

HISTORY

The club organizes many site visits and treks to places of historical significance. These tours are guided ones and different field experts or prominent historians are taken along.

ANC

The Arts and Crafts club, fondly called as 'ANC' in the college is one of the oldest, prestigious and the biggest club of COEP.

BEAUTIES OF COEP


The Main Hall

The main hall is the present day administrative building of the College. It houses the Director, the Dean Academic Affairs, the Dean Student Affairs, the Gymkhana, the Examination cell and other various important administrative heads of the college.

Campus View

The college campus is divided in to two parts by Ambedkar-Wellesley Road, where on the right side lies the Heritage building or Main Hall, Civil , Production and Electrical Engineering Departments. On the other side is the Mechanical , Electronics , Computer-science and Instrumentation departments along with the Gymkhana and Boat club. The campus has enough lush green lawns

Extravaganant MindSpark

MindSpark is the national level, annual technical festival of College of Engineering, Pune. Since its inception in 2007, MindSpark has grown manifold to become one of the best platforms for young minds to think, innovate and showcase their talents.

Read more
Regatta-The Boat Festival

The Regatta is an annual boat festival organised by the students of the College of Engineering Pune. The festival, which has a history of 88 years, is a major attraction for the people of Pune and every year draws crowd in a very large amount.

HOMECOMING

We met here real friends. Everytime since 5 years it is like coming home. Good Feeling is not a hostel - It is a lifestyle!

AMENITIES

  1. The students are provided with basic amenities like cot, table, chair, cupboard in their rooms.

  2. The rooms are spacious, airy, and illuminated with the surroundings landscaped with greenery. Student’s common rooms have TV sets and carom.

  3. The water supply is for 24 hours.

RULES FOR HOSTEL ADMISSION

  1. The admissions are branch wise and on merit basis (CGPA/CET Marks) and as per State Govt. reservation rules.

  2. Merit list will be prepared on CGPA & having no backlog courses.

  3. Ragging within or outside of any educational institution is strictly prohibited.

RULES FOR HOSTEL ADMISSION

  1. Hostel fees for Regular students: Rs. 27,000/- / year / student.

  2. Hostel fees for NRI / FN / PIOs/Gulf: Rs. 90,000/- / year / student.

  3. Hostel Mess deposit for boys (Refundable) Rs. 5,000/-/ student.
